Horror Movies

Horror movies are made to cause intense repugnance, fear, or dread. They may have physical violence and psychological terror; they may be studies of deformed, disturbed, psychotic, or evil characters; stories of terrifying monsters or malevolent animals; or mystery thrillers that use atmosphere to build suspense.

Needs Of Viewers

Using the ‘empathize’ stage of the design thinking process alongside the help of research, we were able to determine that people who watch horror movies need to feel thrill and excitement in their lives, but they also need to feel safe. They feel like they are able to deal with and accomplish things outside of their comfort zone so they feel proud of themselves after.
