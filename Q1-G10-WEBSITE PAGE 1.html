Definition Of Streaming

Streaming is multimedia file such as audio and video that can be instantly played without the need to download it first. A media content, recorded or live, can be considered a streamed content as long as it can be played on any device via the internet even if you do not download it.

How about Live Streaming?

Live streaming is a broadcast that allows you to watch or listen to media content in real time. Your capacity to control how you want to watch it is less than that of pre-recorded content because if you miss something you cannot go back to it.
