Characteristics of a Good Stream

A good stream must have high quality of video and audio with little to no lag. It must be accessible regardless of device. Depending on the type of content, the streamer, if heard or seen, must act appropriately. If it is a gaming stream they must be entertaining and if it is a podcast about something serious they must act accordingly.
