Gaming Content

Specifically, this type of Gaming Content will be focused on the ones where players play the games themselves, oftentimes in a twitch livestream.

Demographic

Opinions

“Watching someone play means that I don’t have to invest my own time ‘getting good’ at the game,”

“I’d rather see someone else who’s good at the game do it.”

To summarize the above opinions, we can see that many people who watch gaming streams enjoy seeing players who excel at the game, so much so that it satisfies their want to experience winning.


“I worry that if I’m actively playing a game, it’ll be too easy to ignore my responsibilities, like my graduate work.”

Watching instead of playing allows them to enjoy the hobby while, say, eating dinner “without feeling like I was going to be super distracted for a long time.”

“Oh, I love video games, but I can’t play them. I got too sucked in!”

To summarize the above opinions, many people do enjoy gaming but do not have the time to fully immerse themselves in actually playing the game as they have responsibilities and priorities that require attention and time. They can multitask doing what they need to do while watching a gaming stream, but they cannot do so while playing themselves.
